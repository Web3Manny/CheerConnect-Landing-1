Quick Answers

Will the emails sound like a robot wrote them?
No. You set your coaching philosophy and tone. The AI adapts to sound like your program. Warm, professional, parent-friendly. Parents will think the coach typed it themselves.
What if my coaches aren't tech-savvy?
If they can leave a voice memo, they can use CheerConnect. You talk, the AI writes. Virtually no learning curve.
How do I know if my coaches are actually using it?
Your dashboard shows your PVR (Parent Visibility Rate): the percentage of your athletes who received a professional update that week. You'll know exactly who's sending reports and who isn't.
Does it work for rec AND competitive?
Yes. Organize athletes by team, age, skill level, or class type. Works for privates, small groups, rec classes, and full competitive programs.
How is this different from texting parents or using ChatGPT?
CheerConnect remembers every athlete's history, matches drills from your video library, tracks sessions over time, and formats everything professionally. It's a system, not a workaround.
What if a parent doesn't have email?
Reports are sent to whatever email is on file. If a family prefers text, you can copy and share the report directly. We're exploring SMS delivery as a future feature.
What about the guarantee?
If you use CheerConnect for 60 days and don't receive at least 5 messages from parents saying something positive about the reports, full refund. Email me, get your money back. No forms, no calls, no guilt.
Can I try it before paying?
Yes. Start free with 3 athletes. See the quality of the reports for yourself, then decide.
